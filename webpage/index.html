Password-Reuse-Manager

We help to organise your reused passwords without storing them.

The open-source PWRM Web Extension is available for Mozilla Firefox. If you decide to store a password, it will be kept encrypted (RSA 2048bits) in your local browser storage.

Source Code: https://gitlab.lrz.de/fuasmattn/password-reuse-manager
